Open letter from JStock founder

Dear JStock users,

Thank you for using JStock.

Last year, I launched a closed source commercial product, JStock Android. This an effort, to enhance the sustainability of JStock - Free Stock Market Software open source project. A portion of revenue generated from JStock Android, will be donated to JStock - Free Stock Market Software project, to support its free and open source development effort. I can use the revenue, to

  • Compensate developer development time
  • Hire freelancers to complete selected development tasks
  • Pay for domain hosting

Having high rating in Google Play Store is essential important. By having high rating, JStock Android can attract more downloads. In turn, more revenue will be generated.

To support the sustainable of JStock - Free Stock Market Software open source project, may I urge you to leave a nice 5 stars review rating in Google Play Store? https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.yccheok.jstock.gui

With higher rating, more sales, I can devote the $, to support the sustainality of JStock - Free Stock Market Software open source project.

Thank you for your support. Together, we just begin.

Regards,

Cheok, JStock founder
